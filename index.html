WHY NOW

China is now the primary operating market for AI and advanced manufacturing — foundation models, compute, robotics, EV and new-energy supply chains, embodied AI and the wider hard-tech stack are all moving at a cadence the rest of the world can no longer observe from a distance. At the same time, governments, associations, industrial capital and industry organisations around the world are actively looking for more structured, continuous ways to plug into the Chinese innovation ecosystem — no longer through one-off delegation visits, but through ongoing working relationships with the founders, investors and operators who actually build.

AGI Villa — the bridge

AGI Villa exists to be exactly that bridge. We are not a Chinese-only founder community; we are an open, inclusive international platform — a stage designed to bring Chinese founders, international operators, policymakers and global capital into the same room, the same conversation, and the same long-term working relationship.

GO SUMMIT 2026 — the bridge, in flagship form

GO SUMMIT is AGI Villa's annual flagship convening — the most concentrated expression of that bridge each year. Under the banner “Just GO”, the 2026-2027 edition runs as a world tour, moving across the four markets that define the next decade of AI and advanced manufacturing: Greater China, Southeast Asia, the Middle East and the West.

Each stop is a two-day programme — SUMMIT + Industry Tour. Day One is the Industry Tour: on-site visits to leading AI, robotics and advanced-manufacturing companies, with face-to-face, deep-dive exchange between the touring delegation and the founders, researchers and operators on the ground. Day Two is the Summit: keynotes, closed-door founder roundtables, investor matchmaking, policy briefings and a signature evening salon.
